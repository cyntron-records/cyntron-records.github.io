About D4NTRIX

D4NTRIX is a Brazilian phonk producer bringing the gritty, hypnotic sounds of São Paulo's underground scene to the world. Known for crafting dark, atmospheric beats with heavy funk influence, D4NTRIX blends the raw energy of phonk with innovative production techniques. His distinctive sound captures the essence of Brazilian street culture and electronic experimentation.
